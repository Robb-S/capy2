Capybara Detector

Take a photo at the zoo or upload an image and find out if it's a capybara. (Instagram screenshots will work too.)

Currently, the Capybara Detector can differentiate among capybaras, rabbits, squirrels, guinea pigs, beavers, degus, chinchillas, nutrias and agoutis.
